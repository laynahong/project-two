Fresh Face Care

It is generally agreed on that there are five different skin types: normal, oily, dry, combination, and sensitive. Learn more about how to identify and take care of sensitive and combination types. Click below to learn more.

Sensitive Skin

Sensitive skin types may show characteristics of dry, oily, and combination skin types but they can also be subject to redness and irritation. Sensitive skin can be a product of skin conditions, but that is not always the case.

Combination Skin

Those with combination skin types have characteristics of two or more types of skin. These skin types often have to use different types of products for various parts of the face and body.
