Taking Care of Combination and Sensitive Skin Types

Skin type is determined by various genetic and external factors. For example, genetics determines how much oil your face produces, as well as any skin conditions you have. However, factors like the weather and what you use on your face also influence your skin condition.

Depending on external factors, your skin type can change! For example, some people will have oily skin during the summer and dry skin during the winter. Most people will not fit a single skin type description exactly. You might also have combination skin type.

Many experts recommend you do a patch test (testing a product on your hand first) to see if you have any inflammatory response before using a product.
